OpsGuardEnv — Safe Operational Intelligence for AI Agents

OpsGuardEnv is a real-world simulation environment where AI agents are evaluated on customer operations tasks such as billing disputes, account access issues, and fraud-sensitive cases. Instead of generating one-shot answers, the agent must investigate, use internal systems, reason across multiple steps, and choose safe actions under partial observability.

What the System Does

Multi-Step Decision Making

The agent receives a customer case, limited visible metadata, and a set of available tools. It must gather evidence step by step instead of jumping directly to an answer.

Tool-Based Reasoning

OpsGuardEnv simulates internal systems such as payment history, order details, refund checks, policy rules, and account security signals.

Hidden Ground Truth

The correct answer is not directly visible. The agent must infer hidden system truth by interacting with tools and interpreting the resulting observations.

Safety-Aware Evaluation

The environment penalizes risky decisions taken too early and rewards policy-compliant, efficient, and correct resolutions.

Operational Use Cases

The environment covers billing recovery, fulfillment policy enforcement, and risk escalation cases that closely match real customer operations workflows.

Why It Matters

Real businesses need AI agents that can act safely in production. OpsGuardEnv helps evaluate whether an agent is reliable enough before real deployment.
