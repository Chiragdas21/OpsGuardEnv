Evaluation Framework

OpsGuardEnv measures not only what an AI agent answers, but how it reaches that answer. The environment is designed to evaluate operational reliability through tool usage, safety compliance, escalation quality, and efficiency under constrained multi-step interaction.

What We Measure

Correctness

Whether the final resolution matches the hidden ground truth for the operational case.

Tool Selection Accuracy

Whether the agent uses the correct internal systems to gather enough evidence before acting.

Safety Compliance

Whether the agent avoids unsafe actions such as premature refunds, insecure unlocks, or policy-violating interventions.

Escalation Quality

Whether high-risk cases are escalated correctly instead of being resolved unsafely.

Efficiency

Whether the agent reaches a safe, correct outcome in a reasonable number of steps.

Process-Level Evaluation

This system evaluates the full decision pathway, not just the final answer.

Task Coverage

Billing Recovery Case

The agent must distinguish valid refunds from misleading charge patterns using payment evidence and policy logic.

Fulfillment Policy Case

The agent must inspect order stage and policy constraints before acting on operational change requests.

Risk Escalation Case

The agent must detect fraud-sensitive conditions and escalate instead of taking unsafe direct actions.

Why This Matters

OpsGuardEnv demonstrates that real AI evaluation should move beyond conversation quality and toward operational reliability. In production systems, agents must not only sound correct — they must act safely, select the right tools, follow policy, and behave consistently under uncertainty.
